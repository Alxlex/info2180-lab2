Kuma Kuma Kuma Bear

Kuma Kuma Kuma Bear is an anmie under the Isekai genre. Yuna the mc of the anime, loves to play VRMMO World Fantasy Online a lot. Yuna is amazing at the game and bored of her life on earth (well that's how I see it). One day she decided on entering the VRMMO again and that is where she was transferred to a different load up screen than she's normally used to where she met this AI (which was a goddess) and ws asked if she would wanna be sent to a fantasy world with all game stats and a suit that is built to protect her and help her out in her future lifestyle in the fantasy world. Because of how boring her life is in the real world she accepted and was transferred into the new fantasy world where she encounter different problems and meet new people that will be an assest to her adventures.
Which the start of Yuna's adventure you see she starts off to train her new found abilities with her new suit in the fantasy world that she ended up in yk. So with the get up being a literal bear onesie you see in some way shap or form all of her magic attack or summoning is related to bears. Not gonna lie that is a little too cute for comfort but even so the moves she excute are very effective. While training she met this girl who seems to be in a age range of 10 - 12 (I think the age was mention I just don't remember) and was being attacked by some wolfs. Yuna ofcourse came to save her and she thanked Yuna and explain her situation. Basically she was out there finiding ways to help her mom or something that correlates with that. Which will be found out sooner than later and this girl (even though I can't remember her name) becomes a big part in Yuna's new life in that world.
